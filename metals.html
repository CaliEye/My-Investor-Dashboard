Antimony — Strategic Sleeper Play
Why Antimony Matters
SUPPLY CRISIS
China controls ~50% of global antimony production and banned exports in 2024. Used in: military bullets, night-vision, flame retardants, batteries, semiconductors. No easy substitute exists.
Investment Angle
REMX ETF PROXY
No direct antimony ETF. REMX (VanEck Rare Earth/Strategic Metals) is the best proxy. Also watch: military/defense supply chain exposure. Long-term 3-5yr hold thesis. Position when REMX pulls back to RSI 35-40 zone.

Solar: Flame retardant in panels
Grid storage: Antimony-flow batteries (emerging)
