    File Articles of Organization NEXT File with Colorado Secretary of State online. Fee: $50. URL: sos.state.co.us → Business → File Documents. Takes 24-48hrs.
  • ○
    Obtain EIN (Employer Identification Number) PENDING Apply free at IRS.gov after LLC is formed. Takes 5 minutes online. Required for bank account + taxes.
  • ○
    Open Business Bank Account PENDING Separate personal + business finances. Mercury Bank (mercury.com) — free, no fees, built for startups/LLCs. Requires EIN + LLC docs.
  • ○
    Create Operating Agreement PENDING Colorado doesn't require this publicly but it protects you. Single-member LLC operating agreement template available free at LegalZoom or Clerky. Documents ownership + management rules.
  • ○
    Registered Agent PENDING Required in Colorado. Can be yourself (if you have a CO physical address) or use Northwest Registered Agent (~$125/yr). They forward legal documents.
  • ○
    Annual Report Filing RECURRING Colorado LLCs file an annual report. Fee: $10. Due each year by the end of the month the LLC was formed. Set a calendar reminder.
  • ○
    Business License (if needed) REVIEW Financial services / investment advice requires licensing. If ChenzO is purely a software/technology company (not giving individual investment advice), standard business license only. Consult a CO business attorney for compliance clarity.
