04 — DECISION FRAMEWORK — WHEN TO ACT
01
WAIT FOR 3+ CONFLUENCE SIGNALS BEFORE MOVING
One signal is noise. Two signals are interesting. Three signals from independent sources — price, volume, macro, sentiment — is a trade. Never act on a single data point. The market is designed to fool you with false signals. Confluence is your filter.
02
MACRO LEADS. CRYPTO FOLLOWS. NEVER REVERSE THIS.
BTC does not move in isolation. It is a risk asset. When the Fed tightens, when credit spreads widen, when the dollar spikes — crypto sells. Check the War Room first. Always. The macro regime determines the crypto playbook, not the other way around.
03
CASH IS A POSITION. PATIENCE IS THE EDGE.
The best trade is sometimes no trade. In a stagflation or liquidity crisis regime, holding cash and waiting for the setup is a strategy — not a failure. The Oct/Nov 2026 target window requires patience now. Do not let FOMO force early entries. The setup will come. Be ready.
