04 — WHEN TO ACT — CONFLUENCE RULES
01
WAIT FOR 3+ CONFLUENCE SIGNALS BEFORE MOVING
One signal is noise. Two is a pattern. Three is a trade. Never act on a single data point — wait for macro, technical, and sentiment to align before committing capital.
02
MACRO LEADS. CRYPTO FOLLOWS. NEVER REVERSE THIS.
Gold, rates, and the dollar tell you the regime. Crypto reacts to the regime — it does not create it. Check the War Room before touching the Crypto page. Always.
03
CASH IS A POSITION. PATIENCE IS THE EDGE.
In uncertain regimes, doing nothing is a strategy. Holding cash while others panic is a position. The best trades come after clarity — not before. Protect capital first, grow it second.
